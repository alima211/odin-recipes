Broccoli Salad

Description

Indgredients

  • 1/2 pound bacon
  • heads fresh broccoli, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • small red onion, sliced into bite-sized pieces
  • ¾ cup raisins
  • ¾ cup sliced almonds
  • cup mayonnaise
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • tablespoons white wine vinegar

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Place bacon in a deep skillet and cook over medium-high heat until evenly brown, 7 to 10 minutes; drain, cool, and crumble.
  3. Combine bacon, broccoli, onion, raisins, and almonds together in a bowl; mix well.
  4. Combine bacon, broccoli, onion, raisins, and almonds together in a bowl; mix well.
  5. Stir into the salad.
  6. Let chill before serving, if desired.
